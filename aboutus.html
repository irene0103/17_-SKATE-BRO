Skate For Life

Bringing together the best of skate culture and street fashion, Route One has been kitting out skaters for over three decades. From seasoned pros to park first-timers, we’re about perfect setups, support and style – for everyone.

Our Story

In 1989, two friends bailed on London life and headed West. Dodging burn out, and ready to make a living doing something they loved, they set up a warehouse in a terraced house in Bristol, and started distributing action sports kit.

Before too long (and after a few false starts) they opened the doors to the first Route One store. Over 30 years and six more stores later, we’ve helped tens of thousands of people dress, feel and skate better.
